This might sound like a weird thing to say, but if you have missing teeth, now is the best time to be alive. Why? Because you have the option to get dental implants, by far the best and most complete missing tooth solution ever available.


They are the only treatment that restores a tooth both above and below the gum line, creating a restoration that is virtually indistinguishable from nature. We use global standard implants from Osstem and Simpladent. If you’re ready to finally fill in the gaps in your teeth, contact us today.


Implant Placement

A dental implant is a small titanium post that is placed directly into the jawbone to mimic the root structure of a lost tooth. Once there, it can be used to support a variety of restorations above the gum line. The procedure to get them all starts with minor surgery that Dr. Vijjaykanth can perform right here at Sriram Dental.



Implant-Retained Crowns & Bridges

An implant-retained crown is an all-porcelain crown that is connected to an implant post using a small metal connector called an abutment, and it’s the perfect solution for someone missing a single tooth. Because the implant has the same basic root-crown structure as a real tooth, it will seamlessly blend into the smile and can be trusted to last for decades to come.

Or, for a patient missing multiple teeth in a row, implant crowns can also be used to support a bridge. Additional prosthetic teeth are bonded to implant-supported crowns to reliably fill in the gap in the smile.


Implant-Retained Dentures

Implant-retained dentures consist of prosthetic teeth that are anchored directly to the jawbone using multiple dental implants, providing a hold a traditional removable denture simply can’t match.


All-on-4

If you could replace an entire row of missing teeth in just three days, would you do it? It’s possible with All-on-4. For this procedure, four implants are placed strategically into the mouth and attached to a denture at the subsequent appointment within three days.
