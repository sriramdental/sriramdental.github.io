Sriram Dental clinic uses AMD Picasso - USA Laser. Lasers can emit an extremely concentrated beam of light. Dr. Shanthimathi can use it to quickly and easily perform a range of procedures without the need for scalpel blades or sutures.

No matter what is used for, it delivers a comfortable experience and reliable results every time.
