What is a Dental Crown?

A dental crown is a tooth-shaped restoration that is fitted over the top of a natural tooth. With one, we can repair a large cavity, fix a broken tooth, hold a weakened tooth together, and even cosmetically enhance a misshapen or undersized tooth.



What is a Fixed Bridge?

For patients missing one or more teeth in a row, a dental bridge is a relatively simple and effective way to replace them. Two crowns are placed onto the teeth on either side of the space in a patient’s smile, and these are bonded to prosthetic teeth to fill it in. If a patient doesn’t have enough healthy teeth to support a traditional fixed bridge, they can also be held in place using dental implants.
