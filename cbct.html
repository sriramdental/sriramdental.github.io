Dental cone beam computed tomography (CBCT) is a special type of x-ray machine used in situations where regular dental or facial x-rays are not sufficient. It is a 3D-scan which produces a wide variety of views and angles that can be manipulated to provide a more complete evaluation.

ORTHOPHOS cone beam computed tomography(CBCT) is commonly used for treatment planning of:

Accurate Placement of Dental Implants with Precise 3D orientation and Pre-planning
SureSmile Aligners with 3D integration
Precise Root Canal treatments with 3D integration
Determining bone structure and tooth orientation.
Locating the origin of pain or pathology.
