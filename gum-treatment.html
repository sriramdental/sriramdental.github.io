Gum Recontouring

Does your gum line cause your teeth to look too short or stubby? This is a very common issue, and it’s one we can easily take care of with our soft tissue laser. With it, we can quickly and painlessly remove excess gum tissue to reveal beautiful, previously hidden dental structure underneath. Dr.Shanthimathi can then artfully shape the new gum line along the teeth to give the smile the perfect frame. Even better? This can all be done in just one appointment.
